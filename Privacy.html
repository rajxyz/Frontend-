Cookie Policy for Toolgram

Effective Date: 06/01/2025

At Toolgram, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and improve our services. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, mobile, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide useful information to the website owner.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the basic functionality of our website. Without these cookies, certain features may not work.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website, enabling us to improve its performance.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enhance the functionality of our website by remembering your preferences (e.g., language settings).
  • Analytics Cookies: We use these cookies to analyze website traffic and usage patterns, helping us improve the platform.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services, such as Google Analytics, to help us analyze website traffic and user behavior. These services may also place cookies on your device.

4. How We Use Cookies

Toolgram uses cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly.
  • Save user preferences (e.g., login information, tool settings).
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve tools and services.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • You can block or delete cookies, but this may impact your experience on Toolgram.
  • Visit your browser's help section to learn how to manage cookies.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Users will be notified of significant updates.
